And that his effort is going to be seen. Surah an najm (53:40)

"And that his effort is going to be seen." This verse from Surah An-Najm (53:40) emphasizes the importance of human effort and the assurance that one's hard work and endeavors will not go unnoticed. It serves as a reminder that our actions and efforts in life are significant and will be acknowledged, whether in this world or the hereafter. This verse encourages individuals to strive diligently in their pursuits, knowing that their efforts will ultimately be recognized and rewarded.
